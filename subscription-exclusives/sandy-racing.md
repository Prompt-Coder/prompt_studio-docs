The **Sandy Shores Boat Racing** is an exciting and dynamic map designed for FiveM servers, offering a thrilling boat racing experience in the scenic desert town of Sandy Shores. This map features a fully immersive racing environment with detailed tracks and surroundings. Below is a comprehensive guide to help you set up and use this map on your server.

***

### 🗺️ Map Location

The **Sandy Shores Boat Racing** is located at the following coordinates:

* **Position**: `2068.81, 3942.30, 33.65`

***

### 🚤 IPL Information

The **Sandy Shores Boat Racing** map is an IPL (Interior Positioning Location) and does not include a doorlock system. You don't need ipl names, they are included in the script.

***

### 🚀 Installation Guide

Follow these steps to install the **Sandy Shores Boat Racing** map on your FiveM server:

1. **Download the Map Resource**\
   Ensure the `promptrace_ipl` resource is downloaded and placed in your `resources` folder.
2.  **Add the Resource to `server.cfg`**\
    Add the following line to your `server.cfg` to ensure the map loads when your server starts:

    ```
    start promptrace_ipl
    ```
3. **Test the Map**\
   Restart your server and visit the map location at `2068.81, 3942.30, 33.65` to ensure everything is functioning correctly.
