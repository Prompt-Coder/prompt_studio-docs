The **Halloween Rave Party** is a spooky and electrifying map designed for FiveM servers, offering a unique and immersive Halloween-themed rave experience. Located in the eerie desert outskirts of Sandy Shores, this map features a vibrant outdoor rave environment with detailed decorations, lighting, and atmospheric effects. Below is a comprehensive guide to help you set up and use this map on your server.

***

### 🗺️ Map Location

The **Halloween Rave Party** is located at the following coordinates:

* **Position**: `2453.19, 3767.47, 41.55`

***

### 🚀 Installation Guide

Follow these steps to install the **Halloween Rave Party** map on your FiveM server:

1. **Download the Map Resource**\
   Ensure the `prompt_sandy_halloween_rave_party` resource is downloaded and placed in your `resources` folder.
2.  **Add the Resource to `server.cfg`**\
    Add the following line to your `server.cfg` to ensure the map loads when your server starts:

    ```
    start prompt_sandy_halloween_rave_party
    ```
3. **Test the Map**\
   Restart your server and visit the map location at `2453.19, 3767.47, 41.55` to ensure everything is functioning correctly.
