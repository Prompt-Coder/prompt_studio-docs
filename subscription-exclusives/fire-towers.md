# 🔥 Fire Towers

These towers provide stunning views and strategic locations for roleplay scenarios, such as firefighting, surveillance, or exploration. ### 🚀 Installation Guide

Follow these steps to install the **Fire Towers** map on your FiveM server:

1. **Download the Map Resource**\
   Ensure the `prompt_gcom_firetower` resource is downloaded and placed in your `resources` folder.
2.  **Add the Resource to `server.cfg`**\
    Add the following line to your `server.cfg` to ensure the map loads when your server starts:

    ```
    start prompt_gcom_firetower
    ```
3. **Test the Map**\
   Restart your server and visit the fire tower locations to ensure everything is functioning correctly.
